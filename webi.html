Kristellar's

Program Structure

Onboarding

Comprehensive virtual orientation to familiarize you with our company culture, tools, and processes. Introduction to your team and project assignments.

Project Work

Engage in meaningful projects aligned with your field of interest. Collaborate with team members via virtual meetings and collaborative tools.

Mentorship

Regular check-ins with a dedicated mentor who provides guidance, feedback, and career advice. Opportunities for one-on-one sessions to discuss your progress and goals.

Training and Workshops

Participate in virtual workshops and training sessions to develop both technical and soft skills. Access to online resources, webinars, and industry insights.

Networking

Join virtual networking events, team meetings, and industry webinars. Connect with professionals and fellow interns to build your professional network.
